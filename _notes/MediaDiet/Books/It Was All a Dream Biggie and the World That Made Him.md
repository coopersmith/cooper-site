*It Was All a Dream: Biggie and the World That Made Him* by Justin Tinsley explores the life of The Notorious B.I.G., detailing his rise from a troubled youth in Brooklyn to an iconic rap superstar, while examining the cultural and historical context that shaped him. Through extensive interviews and research, Tinsley presents a nuanced portrait of Biggie, highlighting both his artistic genius and the societal issues that influenced his life and career.
