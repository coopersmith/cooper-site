**Wet Hot American Summer (2001)** is a comedy directed by **David Wain**. Set in 1981 at Camp Firewood, it's the final day of summer camp before the campers return to the real world. The camp director, **Beth** (played by **Janeane Garofalo**), faces challenges maintaining order while falling for a local astrophysics professor, **Henry** (played by **David Hyde Pierce**), who is preoccupied with preventing a piece of NASA's Skylab from crashing into the camp. The film features various subplots including love triangles, misfits, and a big talent show that resolves the camp's chaotic day. Other notable cast members include **Paul Rudd**, **Amy Poehler**, **Bradley Cooper**, and **Molly Shannon**.
