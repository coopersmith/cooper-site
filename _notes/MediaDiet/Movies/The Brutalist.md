**The Brutalist (2024)**

*Directed by*: [Brady Corbet](https://letterboxd.com/director/brady-corbet/)

*Summary*: Escaping post-war Europe, visionary architect László Toth arrives in America to rebuild his life, his work, and his marriage to his wife Erzsébet after being forced apart during wartime by shifting borders and regimes.
