## Recommended Brands

- **Rittenhouse** — 100-proof bonded rye — high alcohol carries flavor through stirred drinks. Classic Manhattan/Brooklyn workhorse.
  Used in: [[Bensonhurst]], [[Double Rye Manhattan]]
- **Rittenhouse 100 proof** — Same as Rittenhouse — 100-proof bonded. Spicy, robust, holds up in cocktails with strong modifiers.
  Used in: [[Little Italy]]
- **Sazerac** — Aged 6 yr, lower proof than Rittenhouse. Softer rye — less spice-forward.
  Used in: [[Double Rye Manhattan]]
- **Jaywalk Bonded Straight Rye** — Bottled-in-bond Pennsylvania-style rye, grain-forward and dry. Toby Cecchini reaches for it solo where he used to split Overholt + Rittenhouse.
  Used in: [[Other Boulevardier]]
