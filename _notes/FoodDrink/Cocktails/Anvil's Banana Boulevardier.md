Shift drinks kismet occurred when Terry Williams mixed banana liqueur with Campari, resulting in a fruity take on a classic cocktail recipe.

Source: [punchdrink.com](https://punchdrink.com/recipes/anvil-banana-boulevardier/)
Servings: 1

## Ingredients

- 1 ounce [[Bourbon]]
- 1 ounce [[Sweet Vermouth]]
- 1/2 ounce [[Campari]]
- 1/2 ounce [[Giffard Banane du Bresil]]

## Coop's Riff

- 2 ounce [[Rye whiskey]]
- 1.5 ounce [[Sweet Vermouth]]
- 3/4 ounce [[Campari]]
- 1 ounce [[Giffard Banane du Bresil]]

## Directions

1. Stir all ingredients over ice.
2. Strain into a chilled coupe glass or rocks glass with one rock.
3. Garnish with a strip of orange zest.
