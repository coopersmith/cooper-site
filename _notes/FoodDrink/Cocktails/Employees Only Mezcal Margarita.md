Steve Schneider's after-hours Mezcal Margarita at NYC's Employees Only — equal parts simple and rewarding.

Source: [punchdrink.com](https://punchdrink.com/recipes/employees-onlys-mezcal-margarita/)

## Ingredients

- 2 oz [[Mezcal]]
- 1 oz [[lime juice]]
- 1/2 oz [[Agave Nectar]]
- 1/2 oz [[Cointreau]]

## Directions

1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice.
2. Shake until chilled.
3. Strain into an ice-filled tumbler glass.

## Notes

- Companion to [[Mezcal Margarita]] (Adrian's version) — same ingredients, slightly different ratios.
