A whiskey-forward riff on the [[Negroni]] — equal parts whiskey, sweet vermouth, and Campari.

## Ingredients

- 2 oz [[Rye whiskey]]
- 2 oz [[Sweet Vermouth]] (Carpano)
- 2 oz [[Campari]]

## Directions

1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice.
2. Stir until chilled.
3. Strain into a chilled coupe or rocks glass with one large cube.
4. Garnish with an orange peel.

## Notes

- **Toby Cecchini's original variant** (2013, [[Long Island Bar]]) splits the whiskey across two ryes and uses a house vermouth blend:
    - 1 oz Old Overholt Straight [[Rye whiskey]]
    - 1 oz Rittenhouse Rye 100-Proof
    - 1 oz [[Campari]]
    - 1 oz Long Island Bar house vermouth blend (1 part Carpano Antica Formula : 2 parts Cinzano Rosso — combine and refrigerate)
    - Source: [punchdrink.com](https://punchdrink.com/recipes/toby-cecchinis-boulevardier/)
- **Toby Cecchini's [[Other Boulevardier]]** (2024) is a leaner update: single rye (Jaywalk Bonded), Mulassano Bitter in place of Campari, and a Spanish sherry-based vermouth (Barbadillo Atamán) in place of the Italian house blend.
