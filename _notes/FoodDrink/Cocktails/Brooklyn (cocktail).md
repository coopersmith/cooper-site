The dry-leaning predecessor to the [[Manhattan cocktail]] — traditionally made with Amer Picon, often substituted with a rich, dry-leaning amaro.

Source: [cooking.nytimes.com](https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/12529-brooklyn?smid=ck-recipe-iOS-share)
Servings: 1

## Ingredients

- 2 ounces [[Rye whiskey]]
- 3/4 ounce [[dry vermouth]]
- 1/4 ounce [[Maraschino Liqueur]]
- 1/4 ounce [[Amaro Averna]] (or Amer Picon if available)

## Directions

1. Stir all ingredients with ice until chilled.
2. Strain into a chilled coupe glass.
